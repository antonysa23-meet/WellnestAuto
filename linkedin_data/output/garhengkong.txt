Garheng Kong

Founder & Managing Partner of HealthQuest Capital, a growth-capital healthcare investment firm he founded in 2012, investing in women's healthcare providers among other healthcare services.

Garheng holds MD, PhD, and MBA degrees from Duke University, graduating at the top of his class in each program, in addition to undergraduate degrees in Chemical Engineering and Biological Sciences from Stanford University. Previously, he was a partner at Intersouth Partners and Sofinnova Investments, with earlier experience at GlaxoSmithKline and McKinsey & Company, and served as CEO of two healthcare companies.

He currently serves as Lead Independent Director of LabCorp and sits on the boards of Smith & Nephew, Xeris Biopharma, IKS Health, and Duke University's Board of Trustees. He was named Healthcare Investor of the Year at the GrowthCap awards in March 2025.
