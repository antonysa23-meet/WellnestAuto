❤️‍🩹 Saving lives one heartbeat a time. Link to website and to donate below 💫 

❤️ Every two years, our family gathers with an extraordinary community of friends, supporters, and physicians to honor the life of our son, Gunnar. For those that don't know, our son Gunnar passed away at 5 months old from a complex congenital heart disease.

❤️ Last month, we hosted our second Heart Ball in support of The Gunnar Frederick Woelfel Fund. What began as a deeply personal effort to turn unimaginable loss into purpose has grown into a powerful community committed to improving outcomes for children born with congenital heart disease.

❤️ Because of this incredible group of supporters, this year’s event raised more than $900,000 to support research, innovation, and care for children facing complex congenital heart disease. We are profoundly grateful to everyone who attended, supported, and stood beside our family in this mission.

❤️ Congenital heart disease is the most common birth defect, yet research funding remains disproportionately limited. Through this fund, we hope to accelerate breakthroughs, support the physicians and researchers leading this work, and ultimately improve survival and quality of life for children and families facing these diagnoses.

❤️ Gunnar’s life continues to inspire us every day. Seeing so many people come together in his memory reminds us that meaningful change happens when a community rallies around a shared purpose. Thank you to everyone who made the evening possible and who continues to support this mission.

💌 With love,
Sarah & Fritz Woelfel
