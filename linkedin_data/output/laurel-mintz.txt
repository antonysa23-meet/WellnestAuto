About
As founder of Elevate My Brand, I’ve spent 17 years turning bold ideas into measurable growth for over 400 powerhouse brands like Verizon, Paw Patrol, Zendesk, Geico, and Squishmallows. Our marketing strategies don’t just look good—they drive visibility, profitability, and real results.

In parallel, as General Partner at Fabric VC, I invest in the next wave of consumer-tech, health, and fintech founders. With a marketer’s lens and a strategist’s mindset, I help visionary startups scale smart and stay seen. Across both roles, my mission is simple: fuel innovation and impact, elevate bold ideas, and build what’s next.

It's hard to believe how much different this summer will be from my last one. 

Last year, I was heads down in fundraising mode, trying to schedule as many calls as possible with investors before they went off to their summer homes so I could close Fund I strong. The hustle was real! Every conversation felt like a race against time, and the pressure was palpable. With each call, I strategized, prepared pitches, and navigated the complexities of investor relations—all while juggling the excitement and anxiety that come with launching a new fund. 

This year, I am much calmer, taking a methodical approach before launching Fund II. This shift in mindset has been refreshing. Instead of rushing to close deals, I'm focused on building genuine relationships and creating a robust strategy that aligns with my long-term vision. I'm taking the time to reflect on lessons learned from Fund I and ensuring that I’m making informed decisions that will set me up for success moving forward.

For emerging GPs, this is my note to you to let you know that your grind will be worth it in the long run. Keep pushing through the challenges, stay focused on your goals, and remember that every effort counts. Good luck this summer!
