Rebecca Weinberg

Investor at Portfolia, a women's health-focused venture capital investing platform. She also serves as a Women's Health Accelerator Advisor at Springboard Enterprises.

Rebecca has more than 15 years of experience as an operator-investor across corporate ventures, M&A, and early-stage investing in healthcare, consumer wellness, and women's health. She spent eight years at Reckitt, most recently as Venture Director of Reckitt's corporate venture arm, Access VC, working across corporate venture, M&A, and business development. Earlier in her career, she served as Deputy Economic Minister and Director of Business Development for the Government of Israel's Economic Mission.

She currently serves on the board of Maude, and her investment track record includes Beekeeper's Naturals, Future Method, Jude, and Begin Health.
