A model that pays for better patient outcomes, not volume of activity, is the best way to expand access and deliver high-quality, specialized dementia care. Power started with a mission to reimagine trial recruitment with the patient at its core, and to do something about an outdated system that wasn’t serving anyone effectively.

At Power, we’re making it easy for patients and researchers to connect, and data tells us our product is working:

🌐 Over 600,000 patients used Power to discover trials in the last year
