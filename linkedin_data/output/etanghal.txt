About
Deep Tech, Dual Use Companies improving healthcare and security

I am an engineer, entrepreneur, and  venture capitalist and have worked with over 50+ deep tech companies around the world . I started  my career as a device physicist with an  early stage successful semiconductor company. After spending the 2000s as a VC in the UK, I  went on to  be General Manager the VC group of Applied Materials and then Vice President  New Business Ventures Group at ARM - two of the largest global semiconductor companies. 

In 2017, I joined In-Q-Tel (the CIA’s VC Partner) as a Senior Partner then MD of the firm’s London office. The investments I led for  In-Q-Tel  include Cerebras (25x current return), Mythic, EdgeQ , EdgeImpulse (acquired by Qualcomm), Pragmatic and Paragraf.

My successful exits  before InQTel include Sequans(IPO), Liquavista (Samsung acquisition), IMS Nano (Intel acquisition), Pinebrook Imaging (Applied Materials acquisition) and Twist Bioscience (IPO). 

At In-Q-Tel, I also served as an advisor to government leaders  on semiconductor policy starting in 2019 which helped inform the CHIPS act,

In 2022, I decided to start a new endeavor.   My former  MIT housemate  and medical doctor Dr. Tara Bishop and I founded Black Opal Ventures. Our debut $63M fund invests in frontier technology companies improving healthcare and security. I am particularly focused on finding companies that leverage the technology I have seen developed for and by the semiconductor industry combined with advancements in artificial intelligence to advance drug discovery, to improve human longevity, and to strengthen national security.

We have invested in a number of leading companies including:

- Eridu AI (custom silicon provider for AI networking infrastructure) 
- Authmind (identity security 
- Hyro AI (conversational AI for healthcare systems)
- Outpace (AI enabled drug discovery platform for CAR-T therapeutics )
- Parasail (neocloud for AI inference workloads)
- Ansa (enzyme based gene synthesis platform) 
- Blaze.AI (HIPAA compliant no code platform) 
- Oath Surgical (tech enabled ASCs)
- Flytehealth (tech enabled obesity management )
- Conceivable (AI enabled IVF)



I currently serve on the Board of Notre Dame High School for girls  in San Jose.  I am passionate about advancing the number of women in the STEM and venture capital fields.

Josh Benhamou and I have founded Chipper. Fortunately, I had the fantastic idea we needed beaver mascots. Luckily we have great investors who support this.

I have spent my entire professional career in the pursuit of the same mission: Maximize the positive impact of breakthrough scientific innovations. I’m thrilled to announce that we are taking that mission to the next level.

Often a ground breaking innovation's impact is minimized because it stumbles on implementation or fails to be applied to the right problem.

While using AI to speed up innovation is great, I’m an executor. My goal is to get your innovation out of research and into operations, scaled, and purring (fun fact, beavers purr). Because only then does it have a real impact on people's lives.

Josh and I are building Chipper to do just that. Our mission is simple: Bring AI to lab operations in a seamless, non-disruptive way. We are starting with preventing instrument and process failures and going from there.

Our early adopters are already seeing Chipper alerts instead of failed batches. And we’re onboarding new labs. If you are in Biotech, Diagnostics, Pharma, Automation, CRO, … let’s talk!
