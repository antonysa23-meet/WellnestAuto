Shijiro Ochirbat

Principal at Reinventure Capital, a firm investing exclusively in profitable or near-profitable, US-based companies founded by BIPOC and/or female founders. Shijiro leads intake and financial-model analysis and contributes directly to investment decisions.

Shijiro holds an MBA from the University of Sydney and an MPA from the Harvard Kennedy School, with a focus on impact investing, including social finance coursework at Harvard Business School.

Reinventure Capital's investment thesis centers on unlocking capital for founders historically overlooked by traditional venture capital.
