About
Hi, I'm Jessica. I'm an investor and builder and co-founder of Adverb Ventures, an early stage venture capital firm. I have the great professional privilege of helping entrepreneurs bring their crazy, ambitious visions to life. It's a job that never feels like work.

At Twitter, I joined as the 34th company and over 8+ years became the VP of Corp Dev & Strategy. I acquired 30 startups and worked alongside the founders, product, eng, and business leaders to continually push the boundary on the surface area of the company.

I'm a personal angel investor in Gusto, Vanta, Instacart, Contentful, Material Security, Anchorage, Forward, Threads, Color Genomics, Coinbase, Literati, Winnie and many more. As a GP at Google Ventures, I invested in Digits, Instawork, Homebound, Nearby and The Wing.

I'm a Pacific Northwest native and came to California to attend Stanford as an undergraduate, and have been in the Bay Area mostly ever since. I married an Australian and our ultra-running habits have been retired, at the moment, for chasing after two little kids and an Aussie Shepherd at home.… more
