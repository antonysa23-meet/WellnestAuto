3yr • 


As the first granddaughter of Sicilian Immigrants, I saw my family realize the American Dream despite the heavy prejudice they experienced and their lack of education. As a Generation X female, I was told girls could do and be anything. These two influences led me to believe it was not only my privilege, but my duty to pursue a field of study traditionally dominated by men.

I’ve spent the last 25 years breaking barriers and biases in banking, finance, private equity and venture capital. I’ve led teams that have collectively invested $600MM in over a 100 funds and over 40 operating businesses across many industries and company lifecycle stages. My team’s investments generated high ROI and drove over a dozen strategic partnership discussions which led to three acquisitions and six meaningful strategic partnerships/commercialization relationships.

I achieved great success, but at great cost. Corporate America has come a long way in the last two and half decades, but biases continue to run deep and there remains much truth in the adage that women must be twice as good as a man to go half as far.

I started Vree Ventures because my skillset and track record places me in the unique position of being able to drive meaningful impact for female and founders who have received the same percentage of VC funding since I started in the industry during the dotcom boom. Change needs to happen and my team and I are excited to be part of the movement.
