Excited to announce our $3.4M pre-seed led by Long Journey, with participation from Ludlow Ventures, Forward Deployed Venture Capital, Entrepreneurs First, Transpose Platform, Fractal Capital, Ventures Together.

Satellite operators have built something remarkable. 

Nearly 1,000 EO satellites circle the planet, capturing imagery at resolutions unimaginable a decade ago, tracking wildfires, monitoring crop health, mapping infrastructure, guiding disaster response.

But most of that data never makes it to the ground.

The bandwidth to transmit is nowhere near sufficient. And you can't upgrade hardware that's already in orbit.

What you can upgrade is the compression. Modern satellites still use legacy algorithms, but GPUs are becoming commonplace in orbit. Neural networks that learn the structure of the data they compress can do drastically better.

We're building compression that lets operators get multiple times more data down. Same bandwidth. No hardware changes.

The world is sending more sensors into orbit. We're making sure the data gets home.… more
