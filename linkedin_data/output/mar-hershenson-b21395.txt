Sid Malladi

1mo • 

Today we're launching Nuvo's AI-native Accounts Receivable Suite.

Trillions of dollars move through the industries that build our homes, stock our shelves, supply our job sites, and keep energy flowing. Most of that revenue is still collected by hand, through spreadsheets, email, and PDF invoices someone reconciles line by line. Doing it this way traps working capital in unpaid invoices, and it only gets harder as volume grows faster than any team can staff for.

The AR Suite automates the entire cycle. With Nuvo, you can:
• Capture and verify payment credentials at onboarding, cutting fraud, reducing compliance liability, and shortening time to payment
• Prioritize collections and dunning with AI agents that read live payment behavior and risk
• Unify all revenue into a single bank account*, across ACH, wire, card, and check
• Match incoming payments to the right invoices and customers in real time
• Resolve discrepancies with AI proposing the fix and a person approving it
• Sync to the ERP, keeping invoices, balances, status, and exceptions current across every system

The AR Suite runs on Nuvo Intelligence, our coordinated system of AI agents built for the operational work of B2B trade.

With this launch, Nuvo is now the complete AI-native order-to-cash network. Onboarding, credit, and receivables on one system, with agents handling the manual work and people setting the policy and making the judgment calls.

We're building towards a future where the entire physical economy runs autonomously, and we intend to be the infrastructure it runs on.… more
