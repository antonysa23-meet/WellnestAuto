About
We launched avra September 2023. We created avra with one goal - to empower growth stage founders with the right foundation required to scale their company post Series A. We believe the best way to learn as a CEO is from peers and experienced CEOs and operators who have seen scale and company building is dynamic - therefore it is prudent to adapt best practices continuously.

avra runs a 2-month program twice a year. Each week, we cover a different company-building topic, ranging from hiring leaders, to clarity on the event path to becoming a public company one day. 75+ companies have participated so far across 6 batches. We admit ~10-12 companies per batch, keeping cohorts small so every session can be tailored to each founder.

Each session is taught by world-class CEOs and executives running the world's best companies. These experienced leaders provide tactical advice and solutions to founders from their own experiences in scaling their companies. We share our own perspectives based on our experience of working with over 100 growth-stage companies in the last 10+ years. All sessions are closed-door and private.

Prior to avra, I co-led Y Combinator's growth fund (called the Continuity Fund) focused on growth-stage investments, where I led investments in Instacart, Brex, Gusto, Faire, Monzo, Rappi, Groww, Zepto, Whatnot and Boom among others. I am also personally passionate about global technology investing and the convergence of great entrepreneurs across US, China, India and Latin America. I am an angel investor in ByteDance, Ant Financial, Curated among others.  Previously, I was a Partner at Andreessen Horowitz, where I focused on consumer internet growth investments, and worked actively with the management teams of a number of portfolio companies including Airbnb, Instacart, Medium, OfferUp and Udacity.

Prior to Andreessen Horowitz, I was a Principal at The Boston Consulting Group's Private Equity practice in NYC where I led multiple growth equity due diligences in the consumer and fintech sector. I started my career as a senior software engineer at Qualcomm and hold a MS in Electrical Engineering from Virginia Tech and MBA from The Wharton School.
