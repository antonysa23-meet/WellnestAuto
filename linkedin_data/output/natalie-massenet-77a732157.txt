About
Natalie Massenet is Co-Founder of IMAGINARY, a venture fund formed with Nick Brown that backs break-through consumer digital-first brands and retail platforms. The porfolio of over 20 companies  includes Glossier, Everlane, Skims, Kosas, Necessaire, Mejuri and Bread to name a few. 

Previously, Natalie founded and was Executive Chairman of  the luxury fashion online retailers, Net-A-Porter, MrPorter and TheOutnet, served as Chairman of the British Fashion Council, and Co-Chairman of Farfetch. She has been named in Time Magazine’s 100 Most Influential people list, and made Dame Commander of the Order of the British Empire  in recognition of her contributions to the fashion and retail industry.
