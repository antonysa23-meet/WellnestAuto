View Tatijana Janko’s profile
Tatijana Janko

  • 3rd+

Founder of Fjor | investing $300k-400k in enterprise AI and B2B fintech | Host of Allocator Confidential at the Getty and Valin Summit

1mo • 

I pay 4x more for Affinity than Pipedrive. For maybe a 10% better solution. 

That math is the entire story of vertical AI for knowledge work. Generalized copilots and horizontal agents are being built for services and SMB work. Speed, breadth, "good enough." 

Knowledge work is a different animal.

Deal teams, underwriters, lawyers, accountants - their clients pay premium fees and have a long bench of alternatives one phone call away. An agent that's 80% right doesn't save a knowledge worker time. It creates risk. One missed citation, one misclassified entity, one wrong number in front of a client and the relationship is broken. 

The 80/20 rule that powered prosumer and SMB breaks the moment your output sits in front of someone paying for excellence.

That's why the CRM math works (for now).

The winners in vertical AI for knowledge work won't be the cheapest or the most general. They'll be the teams who internalize that accuracy is the product, judgment is the moat, and the last 20% is where the entire fee lives.

One twist I'm watching: small and highly technical knowledge-worker teams are starting to rip out off-the-shelf CRMs entirely and run on a Google Sheet plus an agent personalized to manage the rest. … more
