About
I love connecting with people and welcome new relationships. If we haven't met before, please send me a short note with your interest in engaging as I don’t accept invitations without knowing the intent for connecting. 

I’m a VC (aka knowledge pirate) and capital formation advisor committed to backing founders building new paradigms at the edges of industry, where health, tech, wellness, and culture converge.

I invest in people and businesses that I find inspiring that also make money and move humanity forward. Complex markets with ambiguous regulation is also a specialty. 

If you’re someone that embraces uncertainty, questions norms, and builds new playbooks - not just better versions of the old, we'll have lots to talk about.

My mantra: 
Be bold. 
Be brilliant. 
Be badass.

How can you turn $100K into $10MM+ ? 

I'm running a few experiments and will let you know in 6 ish months...

Last week, I went to Washington DC for the kick off of Recast Capital Accelerate, a program funded by the incredible team at Pivotal Ventures and Melinda French Gates.

JourneyOne Ventures was selected to join as one of the 36 top women-led early stage US VC funds and was awarded $100K, mentorship from top LPs and resources to build our firms.

We invest in frontier wellness industries within misunderstood markets that are heavily regulated and fragmented. We have 7 portfolio companies to date. 

Our Fund 1 focus: 
🌱  Cannabis ($100bn+ regulated and grey market)  
🧠  Psychedelics ($4.5bn 2022 market)
🌎  Global opportunities across software, biotech, CPG, and monetizable IP

Why? 
Our team knows and sees the future, impact and massive investment value of plant based medicines. This year the SAFE bill moved towards a Senate vote for federal cannabis banking reform, and next year, MDMA will be rescheduled for the use of PTSD treatment... We're just getting started. 

This year has been tough... like many of the managers in this program, getting into Recast was a sign to keep building our firms with relentless passion.

Erewhon endcap live 🤝🌿

We’ve quietly become a top-performing supplement brand across a few key categories at Erewhon — so doubling down here was a no-brainer.

But what’s been most interesting is how we showed up.

At Erewhon, we leaned into a more premium, educational design:
→ Highlighting premium ingredients
→ Breaking down functional benefits with zero-sugar
→ Letting the product speak for itself

Because the Erewhon customer wants to understand what they’re putting into their body. They want to see the ingredients and know it's clean (zero sugar, vegan, etc.). We used "Choose Your Wonder" headline to play on our family of products, giving the customer the power of choice....which will hopefully drive a higher basket size.

At a mass retailer, we’d approach this very differently — more callouts, more conversion-driven messaging, faster decision-making.

Same product. Completely different storytelling.

That’s the nuance of building across channels.

And honestly, it’s what makes this fun.

Grateful for the partnership with Erewhon, proud of the Plant People team and our partners at Veriti for executing at this level.… more
