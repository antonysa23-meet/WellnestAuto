Jo-Ann Patneaude

Managing Director, Investor Relations & Fund Administration at Webster Equity Partners, a Waltham, Massachusetts-based private equity firm investing in branded consumer and healthcare services companies, including fertility clinic platforms. She has been with the firm since 2013.

Previously, Jo-Ann held administration, sales, and project-management roles in consulting, venture capital, and professional services, most recently at Atlantic Consultants and Beechwood Capital.

She was educated at Elms College.
