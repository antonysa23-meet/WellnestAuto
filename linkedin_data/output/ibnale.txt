David ibnAle

Founding and Managing Partner of Advance Venture Partners, a San Francisco-based evergreen venture capital firm founded in 2015 investing in digital health, data platforms, and robotics.

David has more than 30 years of experience investing in small and mid-sized growth companies across technology, tech-enabled services, and healthcare. He serves on the boards of Midi Health, Boulder Care, Curology, Alto Solutions, and Morning Consult.

Advance Venture Partners' portfolio includes Conceivable Life Sciences, an AI-powered automated IVF lab that raised a $50M Series A in September 2025, directly aligned with fertility and reproductive health innovation.
