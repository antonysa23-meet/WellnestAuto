About
I invest where real-world pain meets outsized potential.

As Founder & Managing Director of Augment Ventures, I back mission-driven founders using AI to rewire supply chains, logistics, and industrial infrastructure. These markets command >$9 T in global spend yet receive <1 % of U.S. venture capital.

My career began in Mumbai, turning scarcity into scale: early work with Rediff.com and others led to NASDAQ and BSE listings that proved disciplined capital can unlock global ambition. The next chapter unfolded in Ann Arbor, where a seed check into LLamasoft became a $1.5 B exit. Today, 95 % of our portfolio companies secure follow-on funding, and Fund I has already returned cash to LPs.

I’ve seen how operator insight plus venture financing can converts “hard” industrial problems into venture-grade outcomes. This conviction drives every deal I lead. DM me to partner with us.… more

Gina Carfagno

View my services

4w • 

Corporate boardrooms love a binary trap. Every single week, I see leadership teams making the same mistake with agentic AI: they either lock it down until it is useless, or hand over the keys and pray.

Neither extreme is governance. It is a recipe for an operational wreck.
Gartner predicts 40% of enterprises will decommission their autonomous AI agents by 2027. 

The issue is not a lack of cognitive power. It is a total lack of structural enforcement.

Relying on a system prompt is just asking the model nicely to behave. Relying on observability dashboards means you are only analyzing the wreckage after impact.

True enterprise governance requires a deterministic boundary wrapped around a probabilistic actor. The digital workforce needs cognitive freedom, but an independent execution layer must verify and halt non-compliant actions before a single line of code runs.

The winners of the agentic era will not be the companies running the largest unconstrained models. The winners will be the leadership teams capable of proving absolute, verifiable runtime control.

I broke down the architecture required to survive the 2027 countdown clock in my latest article. Read the full piece below.

How is your organization moving from simple AI visibility to active runtime control? Let us discuss.
