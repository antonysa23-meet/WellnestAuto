Amir Blatt

Principal, Venture Investments at Johnson & Johnson Innovation (JJDC), on the EMEA team, overseeing MedTech investments across Europe and Israel and pharma investments in Israel, including women's health devices and digital health.

Previously, Amir was Founding Partner & CEO of Almeda Ventures, the first GP-LP publicly traded healthcare venture capital firm, which listed on the Tel Aviv Stock Exchange in 2020. Before that, he spent roughly a decade at Accelmed, a healthcare venture capital and private equity firm, starting as its first employee and later becoming Partner.

Amir has led investments in nearly 30 companies and served on approximately 10 boards. He holds a BSc in Biotechnology Engineering from the Technion and an MBA from Tel Aviv University's Recanati School of Business.
