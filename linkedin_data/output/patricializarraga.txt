Patricia Lizarraga

Founder & Managing Partner of Hypatia Capital, an asset manager investing in women in leadership since 2007, and advisor to the Hypatia Women CEO Index & ETF (ticker: WCEO).

Patricia has more than 30 years of Wall Street and investment banking experience at firms including Allen & Company, Citigroup, Credit Agricole, DLJ, and Santander Investment. She serves as an independent board member and Audit Committee Chair at Credicorp Ltd (NYSE: BAP).

She holds a BA from Yale University (cum laude) and an MBA from Harvard Business School. She is involved with the HBS Women's Association of New York and serves as a trustee of the Museum of Art of Lima.
