About
Dana is a General Partner and Co-Founder of Vesey Ventures, an early-stage venture capital firm focused on fintech, cybersecurity & enterprise AI. 

Previously, she served as a Managing Director at Amex Ventures, where she led the Enterprise Capabilities investment vertical globally and headed Amex Ventures activity in EMEA as well as the team's overall sourcing strategy. Dana invested in and was involved in the boards of BioCatch, DataGrail, EverC, Menlo Security, Mirato, Signifyd, TrueAccord, Trulioo, and Voca.ai (acq. by Snap). Dana had also led investments is Codat, Melio, Simplee (acq. by Flywire), and Truework.

Dana joined American Express in 2012 and held innovation, strategy and business development roles in New York, prior to joining Amex Ventures in Palo Alto in 2015. Before that, Dana was an Associate at TASC Consulting & Capital, Israel's largest management consulting firm. 

Dana also spent seven years as an officer in Israel's Elite Technological Intelligence Unit (8200). Dana holds a BA in Economics and Management from Tel Aviv University and an MBA from Columbia Business School.

Excited to announce Vesey Ventures’ investment in imper.ai, coming out of stealth today with a $28M raise! 🚀 

GenAI-powered impersonation and deepfake attacks are exploding across video, messaging, phone, and email, creating a security gap legacy tools can’t close. Financial institutions are feeling the heat.

Imper.ai flips the script by verifying the real human behind every interaction. One click in Slack, Zoom, or email, triggers instant, real-time identity checks. No friction, no registration, just elegant, enterprise-ready trust. 
 
We’re backing Imper because the team is exceptional, the approach is truly category-defining, and early traction with financial institutions speaks for itself. In a GenAI-native world, Imper.ai is building the trust layer organizations will depend on, and we’re thrilled to partner alongside Battery Ventures, Redpoint, Maple Capital, and Cerca Partners. 

Huge congrats to Noam Awadish, Rom Dudkiewicz, Anatoly Blighovsky and the amazing imper.ai team!
