Erika Seth Davies

CEO of Rhia Ventures, an organization working at the intersection of racial equity, women's health, and capital. Her appointment as CEO was announced via PR Newswire.

Erika has more than 20 years of experience in fundraising, program design, and racial-equity advocacy. She is the Founder of The Racial Equity Asset Lab (The REAL). Previously, she was VP of External Affairs at ABFE (Association of Black Foundation Executives), where she designed the SMART Investing Initiative, and earlier served as Associate Director of Philanthropy at McDonogh School and Chief of Staff at the Baltimore Community Foundation.

She serves on the boards of McDonogh School and T. Rowe Price Charitable, and on the investment committee of the Nathan Cummings Foundation.
