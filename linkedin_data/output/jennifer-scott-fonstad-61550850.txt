1yr • 

When we launched AI HQ a couple weeks ago — the first enterprise-grade end to end platform for building and scaling agents — a CIO in financial services who I've known for a while reached out to me re one of the demos we showed in the launch event. 

(It was the awesome fund reporting orchestration that Deep Ratna Srivastav and the incredible team at Franklin Templeton built in our agentic beta.)

The essence of our conversation was “If that demo was real – then there’s no way it was accurate/high quality; and if high quality, no way it was as fast as you showed it. Plus, right now, based on what we’re seeing, agentic like that costs more than humans doing the same job.” 

And what I told him privately is what we're telling everyone today — it was the new Palmyra X5 powering the agents that we launched. 

And as of TODAY — that model is now on Amazon Web Services (AWS) Bedrock — our partner in bringing enterprise grade generative AI to financial services and beyond. 

With Palmyra X5 customers are getting SOTA performance 3x faster than other models — and 4x cheaper. 

Why does that matter for agents? 

Agentic work orchestrations requires real-time agentic collaboration between multiple tools, parallel agents working on complex tasks, and massive document inputs without brittle chunking hacks. 

Palmyra X5 can process a full million-token prompt in ~22 seconds and fire off multi-turn function-calls in ~300 milliseconds, while costing 3–4× less than GPT-4.1. Those two numbers—speed and price—unlock agent behaviors that were previously cost- or time-prohibitive.

And a single call can hold every prompt, retrieved doc, and JSON tool response, eliminating brittle chunking logic.

For a while we called it the 'RAG killer' in house  — RAG still incredibly powerful tool in the tool belt but no question for many use cases our approach to this massive context window simplifies the approach for agentic AI. 

Put simply, X5 brings the low latency of a chat model to workflows that involve thousands of pages, dozens of tool calls, and complex memory - Palmyra X5 is the perfect fit for building scalable agents. 

🤝 Thanks to our incredible partnership with @Amazon Web Services (AWS), X5 is available TODAY on AWS Bedrock as well as the Writer platform. 

Learn more about Bedrock here: https://lnkd.in/gVyJRVfu

And thank you Caroline Hyde for having me on today to talk about Palmyra X5 and our partnership with the Amazon Web Services (AWS) team. … more

📣 I couldn’t be more excited to announce the launch of Writer AI Studio, our new suite of development tools that empowers both business users and developers to harness the full potential of generative AI. 

With Writer AI Studio, we're breaking down the barriers that have held back the widespread adoption of generative AI in the enterprise. 🚀

Now, business users who possess the data, know-how, and context can seamlessly collaborate with AI engineers to build AI apps and workflows that drive real business impact. 

For the past 2+ years, our enterprise customers have told us that they get to QUALITY and PRODUCTION faster with Writer than with any other generative AI company, across a broad range of use cases. 

Getting to quality and accuracy is about data, prompt management, continuous upkeep, business context, and COLLABORATION — and with this release, we've made it easy for anyone to have access to these features. 

Writer customers using AI Studio in beta have already deployed hundreds of highly customized AI apps to drive business value. These apps range from knowledge assistants connected to company data to campaign automation apps that generate a suite of launch assets from a single input PPT, to RFP apps that can streamline operational workflows.

Here's a glimpse of what Writer AI Studio offers:

✅ No-code tools: Non-technical users can easily build apps that automate workflows, generate assets, or answer questions using company data. We've taken the hardest parts of building AI apps and workflows and turned them into easy-to-use building blocks that can be used by anyone.

✅ Writer AI Studio API: API endpoints for integrating Writer's generative AI technology into existing apps or services, with SDKs available for Python and TypeScript.

Our customers have already integrated hundreds of highly customized AI apps using Writer AI Studio in beta, things like digital assistants built into a support agent desktop app and end to end campaign management in a CMS app. 

✅ Writer framework: An OPEN-SOURCE framework that enables developers to quickly create feature-rich AI apps with a visual editor and an extensible Python backend. 

We have to get BEYOND the chat app for the majority of AI apps and workflows. The Writer framework is a result of our acquisition of Streamsync, and I'm so excited to officially welcome Ramiro Medina and the Streamsync team to Writer! 

The powerful framework empowers developers to create feature-rich AI apps fully integrated with the Writer AI platform.

Together, these advancements bring the full power of our Palmyra LLMs and the Writer platform to developers' fingertips.

Today is big step forward in our vision to empower people and transform work. Innovation can come from all corners of an enterprise, and generative AI has dramatically reshaped who can be a builder.
