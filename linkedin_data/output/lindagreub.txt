Linda Greub

Co-Founder & Managing Partner of Avestria Ventures, a San Francisco-based venture capital firm founded in 2019 investing in women's health and female-led life-science companies. Across its funds, Avestria's portfolio is 89% women-founded and 61% focused specifically on women's health.

Linda has more than 25 years of experience investing in public and private life-science companies. Her prior corporate development and finance roles include VitaPath Genetics, Novartis Diagnostics, Singulex, and Raindance Technologies (later acquired by Bio-Rad).

She holds an undergraduate degree from Yale and an MBA from Harvard Business School, and is a fifth-generation Bay Area native.
