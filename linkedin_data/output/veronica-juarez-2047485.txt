To set intentions, the conference started with the native American tribe of Ramaytush Ohlone honoring the earth and the sky. We called the four corners...

I was deeply moved by the experience. That powerful ceremony grounded every conversation in a powerful purpose - to be good stewards of the earth. 

During the days of the conference, I met incredible change makers like Mercedes Plendl and Christopher White, and I was reminded that there are thousands of leaders committed to "unlocking the power of markets for impact." It was incredible to see Roy E. Bahat present and, as Roy does, thoughtfully push how we are thinking about impact at scale and that progress demands the inclusion of everyone, not only the people whom we agree with.
