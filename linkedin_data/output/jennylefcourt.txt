About
I have been an entrepreneur, operator, board member, advisor, and investor in tech companies for over 25 years.  I am a General Partner at Freestyle (early-stage VC firm) where I get to invest in, and work alongside amazing founders.  Freestyle is an SF-based firm whose investments include Airtable, Intercom, Patreon, BetterUp, Snapdocs, Digit, Daily.co and many others. I am also a founding member of All Raise, a non-profit dedicated to increasing diversity in funders and founders in tech.

Prior to Freestyle, I co-founded WeddingChannel.com (sold: KNOT) and Bella Pictures (sold: CPI).  I also angel invested and/or advised companies such as Discord, MainStreetHub, Minted and StyleSeat.

Why I’m longer Invisible than Scale AI

Today, Invisible Technologies announced their $100M fundraising round and we at Freestyle Capital are happy to be a part of it.

When we look at the state of affairs in agents today, there is a lot of promise, but less results.

Why is that? If you double click in the AI MIT report showing limited pilot to production conversion and a low % of AI projects delivering true ROI for enterprises, the reasons really aren't that surprising:

- Difficulty getting plugged into the workflows enough
- Need for complicated integrations
- Fear of using a new tool, especially when privacy and AI is concerned

So why am I long Invisible?

That is the stuff this team has been shining at for years and is now scaling up.

The Invisible team has been marrying the best of AI technology and humans long before it was called agentic, and certainly long before it was cool.

They have trained 80% of the world’s GenAI models. Now they are aimed at the enterprise.

70% of the software in the United States is more than 20 years old. If you look at certain industries, like insurance, much of the tech was built even further back than that.

This team knows how to usher real AI results into the world.

Also so refreshing to see a team not raise at overly hyped valuations and focus on growing a real business sustainably and profitably, with massive ambition.

It started with the unique leadership of Francis Pedraza and so many others and is now in the amazing hands of Matthew Fitzpatrick and team. It’s truly hard to find a group this talented who actually care about long term returns and outcomes.

So why am I longer Invisible than Scale AI?

No disrespect to Scale AI as they’ve done something impressive, but we can’t ignore the reality that the existing company left standing is challenged.

They have a great product and some truly talented people, and I know personally how incredible some of them are, but it’s a structural issue.

Given the current valuation they have to grow into from the last round pre-acquisition carve out, it’s hard to attract talent because if you are new to the company you won’t have vested any shares, and now need to start at a revenue-valuation multiple that the company needs to grow back into substantially.

That means the best talent will likely find its way to other places. The talent market has gotten more sophisticated on paper money versus real cash.

Regardless of how it plays out on the competitive front, I’m so excited to be on this journey with great investors like Neil Kamath of Vanara and the Invisible team as we know what they are capable of!

Let’s go! ;)… more
