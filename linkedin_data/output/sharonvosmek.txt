About
An early stage investor, board member and startup advisor for 20+ years.

Architect behind the Astia Expert Sift - Astia's proven approach to investment that greatly reduces bias in the investment screening process. Developed nearly twenty years ago, the Sift is documented, rigorous and proven to screen for the best investments while eliminating individual, network and process bias.  Harnessing the wisdom of the Astia Advisor Community, while muting group think, the Sift is Astia's "golden egg" that consistently sources, screens and selects innovative companies for investment consideration. 

I am well-regarded by entrepreneurs and investors around the globe and known best for my opinions, research and commentary on the importance of women leaders as integral to innovation and high-performing entrepreneurial teams. I have spoken at the United Nations and given lectures at numerous universities on the topic of building inclusive ecosystems.

Post

I recently had the great pleasure of speaking about Astia's #investment activity on the Sustainable & Responsible Investing 360 Podcast with host, Scott Arnell. 

✨ We explored why I believe the venture capital gender gap is not a pipeline issue, but a market inefficiency - one that disciplined investors can address systematically. 

✨  We discussed how the #Astia #ExpertSift methodology was designed to remove individual, network, and process bias from investment screening. The goal is simple: identify the strongest businesses based on fundamentals, not familiarity.

✨ I shared why eliminating warm-introduction gatekeeping materially improves deal flow, how reviewing written business plans before founder meetings reduces pattern-matching bias, and why leading deals with board influence is critical to protecting long-term enterprise value.

✨ Inclusive investing, in my view, is not about concession. It is about a rigorous process and capturing overlooked opportunities.

✨ If you’re interested in how #venturecapital can evolve through structure and discipline, I hope you find the conversation valuable.

Listen to the full episode here: https://lnkd.in/g5DrAfwp

This past International Women’s Day (#iwd2024) , we gathered with the Astia Fund #LPs and #Portfolio #CEOs to get to know each other better and to deepen the ties that bind us. We can see it working in the smiles on our faces.

“A different kind of VC, investing in a different kind of way”

We’ve got this.
For twenty-four years, Astia has carved its own path to source, screen and invest in top performing companies. Why? Because over that same period, our #VC colleagues have chosen a path that systematically failed to include and invest in women. Our intention to #value and #invest in #women meant we needed to find our own way. So we did.

Bringing #Intention to the #Investment Process
From the start, we were determined to be #bold and #intentional in our own investment process as we strategically built the Astia Expert Sift infrastructure. Utilizing #technology, a #community and a robust, data-driven methodology grounded in #research, we deployed best-in-class tools and #wisdom-of-the-crowd techniques to eliminate #bias in our investment process and to identify top performing companies. An approach that has given us a leg up on every one of our investor competitors. We #value women as leaders, we believe in investment as a #force for #change, and we know how to align the two while seeking outsized #returns for our investors.

The Chosen #Partner of #Bold #Visionaries
The results of our strategy speak for themselves and are best demonstrated in the growing #portfolio. A portfolio that demonstrates the world we seek, inclusive of ten companies led by #visionary, #founder #CEOs boldly addressing climate change, inequity in Black maternal health, early detection of ovarian cancer, financial literacy and inclusion, healthcare, creator community empowerment, community investment, and housing for displaced people. The Astia Fund portfolio is built on a high-conviction model that backs today’s CEOs leading tomorrow’s growing companies.

Limited Partners with UnLimited #Impact
By joining us on this journey, our Partners are building a #legacy of impact unlike #venturecapital has seen before. The #innovation ecosystem is forever changed and the #csuite is improved with every investment added to the portfolio. We recognize the road to success for each individual company has just begun, but the innovations they are seeding, the #leadership they are demonstrating, and the #jobs they are creating are here #today.
