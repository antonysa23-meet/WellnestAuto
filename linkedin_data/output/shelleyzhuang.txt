Today we announced a five-year foundation model licensing agreement with GSK to integrate NOETIK's virtual cell foundation models into their therapeutics discovery and development engine. 

This partnership combines GSK's leadership in AI and cancer biology with Noetik's industry-first virtual cell simulation technology. Under the terms of the agreement, GSK will receive a non-exclusive license to Noetik’s OCTO-VC virtual cell foundation model to accelerate the development of novel medicines for non-small cell lung cancer and colorectal cancer. Additionally, we will collaborate to generate bespoke human spatial datasets, applying human-first biological simulation to areas of strategic interest.

This foundation model licensing agreement is among the first and largest transactions monetizing a biological foundation model as a scalable enterprise asset and defines a new asset class in biotech. GSK is now equipped with one of the most extensive oncology multimodal spatial training sets in existence.

Thank you to the entire team at Noetik and our partners at GSK for pioneering this path.
