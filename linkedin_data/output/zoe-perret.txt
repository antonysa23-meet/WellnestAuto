Zoe Perret

Partner at Initialized Capital, promoted to Partner in October 2024 after serving as the firm's fifth Principal since late 2022. She invests in seed-stage founders with interests spanning AI, energy, and frontier technology, and leads Initialized's follow-on fundraising program.

Zoe joined Initialized Capital in 2019 as one of the firm's first operations hires, serving as Head of Strategic Operations. Previously, she worked in capital formation and strategic partnerships at BlackRock and founded the fintech-education startup Advizoz.

She holds a BBA from the USC Marshall School of Business and serves on the board of HousingPlus.
