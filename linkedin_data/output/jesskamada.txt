About
I'm an investor & growth leader. I love growing companies, profitably. 

As a growth operator, I had the honor of spending 8 years building Bamboo, a growth marketing agency where I led our growing client services team & was the head of revenue generation. During my tenure, I had the privilege of leading growth programs for leading consumer tech businesses like Headspace, Peloton, the New York Times & Rover. 

As an investor, I'm building Swizzle Ventures, an early stage Venture Capital firm focused on companies supporting women through life's toughest pivotal life stages. We invest with a growth lens and help our portfolio grow faster. 

As a mom, I'm growing 2 small humans in Seattle with my amazing husband... and experiencing many new pivotal life stages myself! Life brings constant opportunities for iteration.

Funding 🤑  + Data 📊  = Progress 🚀 for Women's Health.  

Policymakers and the business/investment community are converging to catalyze the funding & data flywheel needed for progress in #womenshealth. We came together last week at the NYSE Women's Health Investor Summit. 

Key Data from McKinsey & Company: 
✨ Women's Health is a $1T market opportunity globally. Its a $330B opportunity in the US alone. 
✨ Only 5% of the women's health burden is on women-specific issues. While a whopping 51% of the burden is related to conditions that impact women differently or disproportionally (ie. Cardiac arrest, autoimmune diseases). 
✨ Tip of the research iceberg. Endometriosis is just as prevalent as Diabetes -- 1 in 10 women have it. However, today, we can't diagnose without invasive surgery and there is no cure. 

Mindset Matters: 
📣 "We will build a healthcare system where women aren't an afterthought, but a first thought"  - Dr. Jill Biden
📣 "How can we have actual data in women’s health instead of 'constant self-experimentation'?" - Chelsea Clinton
📣 "Its important to measure progress, but can’t stop until we reach parity (in women’s health funding)" - Chelsea Clinton 
📣 "Treating people equally is not equitable in Healthcare." - Jessica J. Federer

Funding:
💰 $100M in ARPA-H grants will be awarded in the coming months. 
💰$275M Advancing Menopause and Mid-Life Women’s Health Act legislation introduced by bi-partisan senators. 
💰 Venture investing in women's health has increased 350% in last 5 years. 

Women are not small men. Although funding & research have improved, less than 5% of R&D spending is going toward women’s health. The sky is insanely blue with opportunity in women's health. ☀ 

Thank you to Alice Zheng & Jessica J. Federer for co-chairing a magnificent event! So great to see you both!
