We're excited to announce a $4M Seed round led by Game Changers Ventures, with support from incredible investors including Scott “Scooter” Braun, Guy Oseary, Stellation Capital, SignalFire, MaC Venture Capital, and others.

A few years ago, while hosting community events and testing early products, my co-founders Weilyn Chong, Nathan Ahn, and I noticed a consistent pattern.

After every event, people asked the same questions:

Where are the photos? Who captured that moment? How do I reconnect with the people I met?

It all comes back to the reason why we started swsh.
  
How do I see you again, somewhere, somehow.  

Today, brands, teams, artists, and event operators are investing more heavily than ever in live experiences. Yet much of the participation, content, and audience engagement generated at those events remains fragmented, difficult to measure, and largely inaccessible once the experience ends.

swsh is here to help organizations turn live participation into owned content, first-party audience insights, and stronger communities.

We're fortunate to work with partners including Universal Music Group, Warner Music Group, Sony Music Entertainment, Detroit Pistons, LinkedIn, and Forbes. 

Every live experience should have a digital layer that captures participation, preserves memory, and helps organizations better understand the communities they're building.

As live experiences become an increasingly important channel for audience engagement, we're excited to continue building for the organizations shaping culture and community through them.

Thank you to our team, partners, customers, and investors who believed in this vision.

See you somewhere, somehow.
