About
I invest in and work with technology start-ups. I have helped companies define and implement operations, sales and marketing strategies, and have worked with them as they test their business models and raise capital. I have worked with Saas, e-commerce, marketplace, and application development businesses and have experience in management, operations, business development, growth, account management, product management, project management, and fundraising. 

I have an MBA from INSEAD and have worked in Canada, Brazil, and the United Kingdom.
