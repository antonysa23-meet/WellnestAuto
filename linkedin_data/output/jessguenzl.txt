Jess Guenzl

Investment Committee member at Coyote Ventures, a San Francisco-based seed-stage digital health fund founded in 2021 focused on investing in overlooked health populations, especially women's health.

Her primary role is Chief of Staff at Eon.io. Previously, Jess worked at 137 Ventures and at Mousse Partners, a family office investing across private markets.

Her background spans venture investing and operating roles at the intersection of technology and finance, bringing a cross-functional perspective to Coyote Ventures' investment decisions in the women's health space.
