One of the biggest no-brainers this year was backing Francois Ajenstat and Golden Analytics. 

I don't normally write massive "why we invested" pieces, but there are some really important reasons why Golden is personal to me. 

It's no secret that my path into venture was unconventional. I didn't come from investment banking or consulting. I didn't have a shiny MBA. I learned the business from the ground up, and that included making my way around data rooms. 

Data analytics is core to business evaluation. At my old firm, our primary process involved good ol' Tableau. We had one seat, and everyone would slot time with someone on our Research team to slice and dice the data and serve up observations and learnings. 

It was an enormous bottleneck, and as we know in venture, speed wins. 

Fast forward, now running my own firm, AI is changing the way anyone within a company can achieve massive leverage, and there's no longer any gatekeeping to data analysis. 

AI is leveling the playing field. 

All of this is true in venture, but it extends to every sector, no matter the size and scale. Teams big and small will be better, faster, and stronger now that Golden exists - myself included!

Congrats to the Golden team on their big debut today! 

Extremely grateful to be investing alongside Madison Faulkner @New Enterprise Associates (NEA) & Tim Porter @Madrona.
