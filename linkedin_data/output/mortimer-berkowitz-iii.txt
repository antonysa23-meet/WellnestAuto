Mortimer Berkowitz

Founder & Managing Partner of InnovaHealth Partners, a medtech-focused growth buyout private equity firm with approximately $900M in committed capital.

Mortimer has more than 33 years of healthcare private equity investing experience. He co-founded HealthpointCapital in 2002 and earlier founded BPI Capital Partners, a healthcare IT-focused private equity firm.

He currently serves as Lead Director of Alphatec Spine and Channel Medsystems, Chairman of Koelis SAS, and as a board member of Radiaction and Monteris Medical.
