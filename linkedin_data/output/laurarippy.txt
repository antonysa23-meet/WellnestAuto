Laura (Bordewieck) Rippy

Managing Partner & Board Member at Alumni Ventures, where she runs multiple sub-funds including the Women's Fund, The Yard Ventures (Harvard-affiliated), Green D Ventures (Dartmouth-affiliated), and the US Strategic Tech Fund.

Previously, Laura was a serial CEO, Chairman, board member, and advisor across high-tech companies, including Microsoft.

She holds an MBA from Harvard Business School and an AB in Government from Dartmouth College. Laura was ranked among Business Insider's "Best Early-Stage Investors" in both 2024 and 2025.
