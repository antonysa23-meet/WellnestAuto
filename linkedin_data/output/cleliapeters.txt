About
I am the Founding Partner of Era Ventures, bringing a decade’s worth of investing experience to founders building companies looking to transform our physical world. 

I am passionate about doing things better, exploring new ideas, and doing business in a way that is both effective and humane. I believe strongly in the power of women to shift paradigms of leadership and success in the 21st century.

Featured
Post

I am so proud to announce the public launch of Era Ventures Fund I, an $88M fund dedicated to transforming our physical world! 🏙️🚀

I have spent a decade of investing in transforming the physical world, from co-founding one of the first specialist funds to being a Venture Partner at a leading generalist VC, and I've seen firsthand the need for a new approach. Era Ventures isn't just about incremental improvements - we're backing founders with the vision and grit to fundamentally reimagine how we build, manage, and experience our physical environments.

It’s our mission to catalyze the long-overdue disruption of physical world industries (including real estate, industrials, logistics and more). We're investing across stages in game-changing technologies and radical ideas that will shape the future of how we produce, manage and sell goods and spaces in the physical world. 

It’s one of the joys of my life to do this work surrounded by people I respect (most notably my incredible partner Raja Ghawi and the amazing Leanne Vaccaro). I am deeply grateful for the support of our LPs, including New York Presbyterian Hospital, ICG Advisors, BentallGreenOak, Bain Capital Ventures, and many others who believe in our vision.

Most of all I am grateful to the extraordinary founders who have already trusted us to be their partners as they build transformational companies. Thank you Troy Harvey (PassiveLogic), Alec Hartman and Marc Hartman (Welcome Homes), Justin Levine and Mo El Mahallawy (Shepherd), Santiago Bueno (Latii), Brian Hardecker (Truehold), Gregg Ratanaphanyarat (ViaBot), Vishwas Prabhakara (Honey Homes), Duncan Jennings, Max Jennings and Richard Foister (Ostrich), Shaival Shah, and Tim Heyl (Homeward)

We bring this fund to the world with both rigor and care. We seek to engage with humility, kindness, and diligent curiosity, recognizing that the best ideas often come from unexpected places. If you share our vision for a transformed physical world, please reach out.

A shift to digitization has led to hyper-efficiency in some areas of real estate, but it's lopsided. AI will optimize the whole process, top to bottom: (more below) https://lnkd.in/eSzrZagp

Think of the real estate and proptech world as a chain.

Any mistake affects the next stakeholder and the end result: the building
E.g. Delayed planning -> slow construction -> costs balloon -> fewer units -> delayed move in

Lopsided advancement in one area is everyone's problem.

AI will smooth out each link in this chain.
1. Autopilots speed up permitting, pre-construction. Any task with a large corpus of data (visual, language, numerical)
2. Automation -> bundling of tasks -> enhancing productivity + efficiency
3. Streamlined handoffs

The result: fewer errors, better results, and greater operational efficiency at every individual link. 

Taken together, that's a productivity cascade across the whole chain. 

This is just the beginning. We at NFX dive into several niches of opportunity here: https://lnkd.in/eSzrZagp… more
