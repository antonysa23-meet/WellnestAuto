About
As the visionary Founder & General Partner of Delta Blockchain Fund, Kavita Gupta has been at the forefront of the blockchain revolution, steering the industry towards practical, transformative applications of decentralized technology. Her journey in investment spans nearly two decades, including pivotal roles with global impact at The World Bank and the International Finance Corporation. She's recognized for founding ConsenSys Ventures, pioneering early blockchain investments, and for her strategic advisory roles across prominent institutions like the Center for Capital Markets Competitiveness and the International Emmys. An acclaimed educator, Kavita enriches minds as a visiting scholar at Stanford University with her "Beyond Bitcoin" course. She's a lauded speaker, shaping discourse in cryptocurrency and blockchain, and a UN Innovation Award honoree for her work in green and social impact bonds. Her academic prowess is backed by a Master’s in International Trade and Science from The George Washington University, complementing her Bachelor's from Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University.
