Modernizing Banks & Credit Unions Operations with AI | Founder/CEO Saris AI | Founder Ready Education (YC S16, acquired) | Forbes 30under30 | YPO

1mo • Edited • 

I couldn’t be more excited to share that Saris AI has raised a $28.8M Series A, led by Alex Kolicich from 8VC.

We founded Saris around a simple conviction: the future of financial services is humans and AI working side by side. Not AI replacing people, but AI removing operational burden so humans can focus on what they do best.

Over the last few years, the Saris team has been obsessively close to our customers, mapping workflows, understanding daily operations, and doing the actual work across lending and compliance. I still fondly recall the week-long onsite sessions with our early customers, and the late-night iterations (shoutout to Alice, James, Tommy Endres, Julien Andreu, and Clément Flodrops).

One recent story to highlight why Saris matters:
• A credit union is using Saris to process and fund ~$45M in consumer loans every month. Employees use Saris an average of 4.46 hours per day. Loan packages that took 45–90 minutes are now completed in under 7 minutes. Members get funded faster, they have a delightful experience, and operational efficiency improves!

What’s next:
• Creating the standard: Today’s AI playbook in financial services is suboptimal. Most companies build point solutions, extend SaaS offerings, or provide sandboxes. This leaves teams to spend months/years managing integrations, maintain infrastructure, inference costs, and proving ROI. We rejected that model. This is a feedback loop without outcomes in mind. We are building a foundational engine that gives financial institutions a unified way to run agentic workflows across lending, compliance, and operations. Point solutions are easy. Building a seamless system that unifies humans, AI agents, infrastructure, data, and regulatory interactions is not. That’s the standard we’re building towards.

• Customer first: Everything we build is ultimately about helping our customers better serve their communities. They are the heroes of this story. The more time I spend with banks and credit unions, the more I fall in love with the mission, whether it’s helping a hardware store in rural Mississippi secure financing, supporting an agriculture loan in Illinois, or helping a young family buy their first home.

·  Growing the team: If you love to solve real world problems using agentic AI at a scale that impacts millions of people, come join us!

I’m incredibly grateful to my cofounders (Alice Dinu and James Dang), the Saris AI team, and our amazing customers.

Founder @ Quill - Personal, Private, Post-Meeting AI

4mo • 

A great conversation is often the best experience of your day.

But what brings us down to earth afterward? All the mundane work we have to do coming out of a great, dense conversation. Everything we have to remember. Everything we said we’d get to someday but inevitably let slip. A single conversation can spawn many tasks - and most of us have more than one conversation each day!

Today we're announcing Quill Meetings's evolution into your personal Chief of AI Staff and a $6.5M seed round led by Basis Set Ventures and 500 Global to make it real.

Quill started as a private, local-first AI notetaker that gave users control over how their data is processed and stored. That foundation of respect for users hasn’t changed. What has changed is what Quill can help you do with all your context.

Autonomous AI agents are like coworkers who are great at taking direction and doing specific jobs but who you wouldn’t trust to run the company. The human role is becoming making decisions, setting direction, and making connections. More multi-tasking, more responsibilities. Engineers are already living this - now when I code, I’m typically managing 5-6 agents at the same time and thinking about how to guide them, QA them, and improve them.

Quill is built to help you scale your capacity in this world.

With Quill, you can ask "Who should I introduce Julian to?" and it will recall a conversation from months ago, cross-reference your Airtable CRM for relevant investors, and draft the intro emails. It listens to your engineering standup and suggests updating two specific Linear tickets. It learns how you write sales follow-ups and starts drafting them before you ask.

Before he joined Quill, Clayton Bryan fed a year of Quill conversations into a digital clone that now coaches startup founders on his behalf with just a phone call and an understanding of his unique point-of-view.

Because Quill’s users own their data completely, power users are already building their own automations on top of Quill. Enterprises in regulated industries like financial services, deep tech, and healthcare are deploying it inside their own networks with zero data leaving their control.

This is what we mean by a Chief of AI Staff. Not one more generic summary. An AI on your side that works the way you want, that earns your trust, and that helps you stay centered in an increasingly complex world.

Every one of our investors was a user of Quill before writing a check. We’re live, we’re hiring engineers, and we’re just getting started.… more
