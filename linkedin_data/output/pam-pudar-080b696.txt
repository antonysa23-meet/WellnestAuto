Pam Pudar

Limited partner of Belle Michigan Impact Fund (BELLE Impact Fund), a Grosse Pointe Farms, Michigan-based venture capital firm founded in 2012 that invests in women-led companies.

Pam's professional background is in automotive and corporate finance. She served as Director of Finance at General Motors, and previously held roles at ISG and TPI as a Principal Consultant.

Beyond her finance career, Pam coaches entrepreneurs and teaches through Cooking Matters, bringing a practical, operator-informed perspective to her involvement with women-focused investing.
