About
I’m a Founding Partner at FoundersEdge, a pre-seed venture capital fund backing experienced founders building AI-native companies designed to last.

I’m also an exited founder, which means I care deeply about how companies are built, not just what they raise. If you’re an early-stage founder thinking about product, traction, or fundraising - this is the work I love most.

At FoundersEdge, we invest early and surround founders with a hands-on network of experienced operators, multi-time founders, and investors who help with:
- Go-to-market (GTM) experiments and validating startup assumptions
- Preparing for fundraising, acquisitions, and exits
- Product and user experience decisions
- Navigating the emotional ups and downs of startup life

Want to pitch us? You can submit your startup deck at FoundersEdge.com, we review every one.

Beyond investing, I’m especially focused on getting more women on both sides of the table - founding, funding, and exiting.

In October 2024, I started UpLevel, a women founders & investors lunch series.
- 20 curated lunches
- 180+ founders & investors
- 7+ cities

These relationships have led to real investments, partnerships, customer intros, and long-term collaboration. We now host an annual UpLevel Summit to compound that impact and unlock the full power of the network.

Previously...
- Worked with Guidely, Suggestic, and Runzy who acquired IP developed by the start-up I founded in 2018, Wishroute.
- Founded Wishroute
- Worked in consulting at PwC
- Became a licensed CPA
- Got my MBA (valedictorian) and my Masters of Science in Accounting (valedictorian) from Babson
- Married the love of my life, Conor Lynch

If you’re building something ambitious share your deck at FoundersEdge.com, or want to collaborate, feel free to reach out here on LinkedIn.

Welcome Gregory Raiz, Co-Founder & General Partner at FoundersEdge on this episode of 🔥The Lantern 🔥

FoundersEdge is a Boston-based pre-seed venture fund built by exited founders, investing in early-stage teams using AI to disrupt traditional industries. 

🔍 What is FoundersEdge actually building? 🔎

Greg and his partner Jess Lynch are running a data-first pre-seed fund with a data driven, hands on approach. 

They're scraping signals from across the venture ecosystem to make sharper investment decisions, then turning that same data back into operating insights for the founders they back. 

They think there’s a lot of noise in early-stage investing and give founders a real, data-driven edge on Go-To-Market, ICP, and growth.

💡 What kind of Founder is FoundersEdge backing in 2026? 💡

The pattern Greg keeps coming back to: founders with deep operating experience in a target market, often a "boring" one, who are now using AI to disrupt it. 

FoundersEdge has the data to show that founders who understand their market best consistently outperform. Domain expertise plus AI-native thinking is the wedge they're looking for right now.

🤔 What's it like building a venture fund as a former founder? 🤔

Starting a fund is essentially Founder mode all over again. 

Greg has been re-examining everything from first principles. Define your core values, your thesis, your ICP, and your unfair advantage for sourcing and winning deals. 

Just like a startup, the best venture funds find their differentiation early and lean into it from day one.

🤝 How can we help FoundersEdge? 🤝

It’s all about Founders helping Founders. 

Greg and Jess are building community across Boston & New England, NYC, and SF at dinners & their event series Ditch the Deck. 

If you're a founder, the ask is to lean in: try other founders' products, give honest feedback, share what you've learned. 

Go check them out, and give Greg a follow on TikTok while you're at it!
