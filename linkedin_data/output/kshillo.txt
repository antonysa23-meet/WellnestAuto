CEO of Winslow | AI-native payroll, benefits and people ops for modern teams |  Build with us

Visit my website

5d • Edited • 

Nobody has ever loved their HR software.

When we talked to 250+ HR professionals this year, it became clear that legacy platforms are clunky, rigid, and built to create work, not remove it.

The same wish list came up over and over. 

- Get my time back from busywork.
- Let me build what my org actually needs instead of waiting on someone's roadmap or begging IT for resources and budget.
- Kill the friction my employees feel at every turn.

So today Dalton Wais and I are launching Winslow — the first truly AI-native platform for payroll, benefits, HR, and operations.

We set out to design HR software that would let HR teams actually build what they wanted. Along the way, we realized Winslow could be so much more.

It does everything you'd expect - payroll, benefits, employee data, workflows. But Winslow is also an AI employee, available to your whole team 24/7 in Slack, Claude, Codex, and our app. Ask for what you need - it gets done.

→ Want a Slack ping every Monday with the week's birthdays and work anniversaries? Just ask.

 → Onboarding a new engineer? Drop their offer letter into Claude and Winslow takes it from there.

 → Built a dashboard in Lovable using your Winslow employee data? Share it with your team right inside the app.

All of it runs on the most robust MCP toolset out there, wrapped in a deep permission and security layer on SOC 2-compliant infrastructure.

The goal was simple and a little audacious: rethink HR and payroll not as something that could suck less but as something every employee might actually fall in love with. 

It's pretty awesome. And honestly? It feels a little magical.

None of this type of thing is possible alone. Thanks so much to huge investor support along the way from Mark Linao from Progression, Erin M. Faverty, MA COML and Cara Brennan Allamano at PTP, Frazer Anderson Dave B. Blundin and Camilo Román Cepeda at Link Ventures, Ben Casnocha and Lindsay M. Pettingill at Village Global, Kate Beardsley and Jessica Peltz-Zatulove at Hannah Grey VC, Brian Shin at VitalStage Ventures, Stu Smith for doubling down, and countless other angels, advisors and critical figures like Adam Lurie who’s been ride or die through the whole process, Elaine Davis our OG advisor, Elliott Dykes our relentless supporter, Brett Ungashick who got it before almost everyone else, and Jessie Schofer who we've had more spirited convos with than anyone else.

We can’t wait for everyone to try Winslow out. Whether you need to run your first payroll or you’re 50 people and running from the crazy prices of a PEO, we have your back. Feel free to reach out on the website or here.

For today, if you like what you see please give us a repost, comment or some love.

To build is to manage risk. Every construction project is a risk management exercise.
 
Commercial risk. Contractual risk. Financial risk. Operational risk. Yet despite generating enormous amounts of data and experience, our industry still struggles to learn from it.
 
Too often, critical knowledge remains trapped in documents, reports, contracts, emails, drawings, and project files. Lessons learned on one project rarely inform the next. We keep reinventing the wheel.
 
We built Enlaye to change that, and today we’re proud to announce our oversubscribed $5M Seed round.
 
Beyond funding, this milestone is validation that the construction industry is ready for a fundamentally different approach to risk management.
 
We’re building an AI-native Risk Lifecycle Management™ platform that helps contractors, developers, and owners transform fragmented project information into actionable risk intelligence across the full project lifecycle, from preconstruction through delivery.
 
We’re grateful to the customers who trusted us early, the partners who opened doors, the advisors who challenged us, and the investors who believed in our vision. Thank you to Glasswing Ventures (lead), Link Ventures (co-lead), Imad Ventures (strategic) alongside AiSprouts VC, Hannah Grey VC and Apprentis Ventures, and everyone who has joined us on this journey.
