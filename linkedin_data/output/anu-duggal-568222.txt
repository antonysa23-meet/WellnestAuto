Anu Duggal

Founding Partner of Female Founders Fund, launched in 2014 and now on its third fund, having raised approximately $125M from limited partners including Melinda French Gates' Pivotal Ventures and Goldman Sachs.

Anu began her technology career as co-founder of Exclusively.in, an e-commerce company backed by Accel and Tiger Global that was later acquired by Myntra.

She was named to Fortune's "40 Under 40" list in 2018. Her investment portfolio includes Eloquii, Zola, Billie, and Co-Star. Fortune profiled her in March 2025 on the topic of adversity and investing in women-led companies.
