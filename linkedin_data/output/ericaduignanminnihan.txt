About
We’re not just building startups — we’re building the future. As Founder and General Partner at Reign Ventures, I back visionary founders who are creating what the world doesn’t yet know it needs. My mission is to unlock generational wealth and cultural breakthroughs by investing in seed-stage startups led by bold thinkers building at the edge.

With over 20 years of experience across Wall Street, entrepreneurship, and early-stage venture, I’ve seen firsthand how access, capital, and belief can transform trajectories. At Reign, we pair high conviction with deep founder empathy and an unmatched network to empower game-changing companies from day one.

Reign Ventures Fund II is our next step in scaling this vision—and I’m here to connect with aligned partners, co-investors, and founders who believe the future belongs to the brave.

The $2.2B problem no one has solved. Today, we did. 🔥 

Since 2021, the SEC has fined 100+ firms over $2.2B for communication violations. Every single case was caught after the message had already been sent. That is still how the industry operates: send first, pay for it later.

Now add AI agents generating thousands of messages a day on behalf of your company. No human review team can keep up. Before LLMs, stopping this before delivery was impossible. Rules alone could not understand tone, intent, and context at speed and scale.

Today we're launching the ZeroDrift API for enterprise developers.

One API call checks every message and every AI output against regulations such as SEC, FINRA, HIPAA, FCA, GDPR, and more, plus your firm's own internal policies, before anything leaves your organization. If something violates a rule, ZeroDrift flags it, cites the regulation, and rewrites it in real time before it is sent.

Under the hood, deterministic rules catch hard violations with precision. LLMs catch what static systems never could: tone, intent, context, and suitability. Together, they turn compliance from reactive review into real-time enforcement at a fraction of legacy cost.

Every compliance tool before this was built to watch the damage after it happened. We stop it before it happens.

We are defining a new category: communication enforcement.

If your firm is deploying AI agents, chatbots, support assistants, onboarding assistants, automated email agents, voice agents, or any other customer-facing AI tools, you need a compliance firewall. Every outbound message carries regulatory, legal, and reputational risk. ZeroDrift stops that risk before it leaves your organization. We can get you live immediately.

Message Yuedong Lu tony@zerodrift.ai, cc kumesh@zerodrift.ai to fast-track demos and onboarding.

Big shout out to Anand L.. for leading this development and to a16z speedrun (Andreessen Horowitz) for believing in our vision from day zero.
