Maitree Mervana Parekh

Principal at Acrew Capital, focused on health and fintech investing. Her portfolio work includes investments in Cometa, Future Family, Banked, and Creative Juice.

Previously, Maitree was an investor at Cerity Partners Ventures (formerly Touchdown Ventures), where she helped launch corporate venture capital programs, including one for T-Mobile. She began her career as an investment banker at Citigroup.

Maitree holds a BA in Business Economics from UCLA. She was named to the Forbes 30 Under 30 list (Venture Capital category) in 2022, serves as a board member at NextGen, and is active with All Raise, supporting the advancement of women in venture capital.
