It almost can’t be said more incisively than this about defense acquisition: by minimizing local risk, we’ve maximized systemic risk. There’s that, and a lot more, on this week’s CTRL+ALT+DEFENSE, as Matt Cronin of Andreessen Horowitz joins Meagan Metzger and Billy Mitchell to talk about how America can revitalize its industrial base through smarter partnerships between the DoD, venture capital, and emerging tech.

Indeed, rules designed to force compliance and scrutiny on any particular contract lead to a place where the defense enterprise, in general, can’t adapt to a world of fast-moving threats.

Matt explains how a16z’s American Dynamism practice is tackling that by both betting on companies that are rebuilding critical national capabilities – from manufacturing and cybersecurity to defense systems – and advocating for policy reform to finally unlock those capabilities for the American people.

🎧 This one is a roadmap for how DoW and the venture community can work together to make American defense faster, more resilient, and more competitive. Check out the conversation below!
