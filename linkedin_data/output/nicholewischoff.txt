Over the past 2.5 years I have put my head down and focused on what I know to be true - there are few fantastic dedicated early stage firms and even fewer that you have ever heard of. Perhaps this firm could be both.

I set out in March to raise a $50M fund focused on leading pre-seed and seed stage investments. Just shy of five months later and I am elated to share that I have closed my third fund. This brings the firm to roughly $80M in AUM in under three years. To date, I have been alone in building Wischoff Ventures. I am thrilled to welcome Neal M. as a Senior Associate based in NYC.

To my founders, I wake up every day fired up that I get to do this job and to spend my time with you. And to those of you building that I haven't met, I can't wait to meet you! 

I wouldn't be here without the support of Chad Byers and Blake Byers. My first phone call since 2021. Lee Fixel, a mentor and quiet killer. Cendana Capital who has anchored all three funds with unwavering belief in me and my plans. I went far more institutional on the LP front this fund welcoming in Children's Health, Churchill Asset Management and Screendoor to name a few. I can't wait to continue to deepen the many new relationships. 

I have shared (and likely overshared at times) as much as I can online about the every day of building this firm. To those that have been here from the beginning, thank you. Still just getting started.

Thank you so much to Alex Konrad for the great write-up.
