About
The future of AI is being built now. I invest in the founders making it happen, before the rest of the world sees what’s possible. 

As a longtime investor in AI, I back early-stage founders working on transformative applications of AI in real-world domains—before their ideas become obvious to everyone else. I invested in 3 AI Unicorns well before the launch of ChatGPT.

My perspectives scaling and selling a startup (Andiamo, acquired by Cisco $1.5bn) and building early ML products at Skype (acquired by MSFT $8.5bn) brings hands-on practical operating experience to my founders beyond just capital. And I leverage the best ideas from top tier venture firms like Khosla Ventures - where I was a Partner for 4 years - to help founders at the very earliest stages, all the way through IPO.

I believe AI’s future belongs to those who democratize it, not monopolize it. The best founders I work with think about not just breakthrough technology, but how to build great products for 8BN people globally, not just 80M of the elite. They build insanely useful and usable products that unlock latent demand and transform the way we work and live.

I call this AI for the Real World.

If this is your vision, too, let’s connect!

So proud to back Josh Sirota Eragon - the modern AI OS for the workplace!

At Axiom, we talk a lot about "AI for the Real World." Not demos. Not toys. Building insanely useful and usable products for 8BN people globally, not just 80M elite.

Eragon is exactly that.

Josh has a bold thesis: software as we know it, is dead. Buttons, drop-downs, dialog boxes - all relics. Writing code to access an obscure database - ancient history. The future of enterprise work is a simple query, a natural language interface that replaces your Salesforce, your Jira, your Tableau interactions. You ask. It does. And, in the future, it just does.

The implication? Anyone can understand how THEIR business is doing in real-time, and make decisions quickly - without the need for data science teams, developers and complex IT systems.

Eragon trains models on YOUR company's own data, deploys them in YOUR own environment, and gives YOU ownership of the model weights. That's not just a feature — that's a philosophy of personalization and empowerment.

This maps directly to what we believe at Axiom Partners. The world's biggest problem isn't a lack of AI capability. It's that the most powerful tools remain out of reach for most people and most businesses. Elite enterprise software has always been expensive, complex, and locked behind armies of implementation consultants.

Eragon tears that down. If your entire software stack is accessible via a natural language prompt, suddenly the playing field levels. A 10-person startup can operate with the same intelligence infrastructure as a Fortune 500.

That's AI for the Real World.

Congrats to Josh and the Eragon team. This is just the beginning. 🚀… more
