About
Venture investor and strategist with significant experience in VC, PE, management consulting, and impact investing as well as operations. Proven track record of high-quality strategic work and operational expertise in a wide range of markets and industries. Focus at the intersection of technology and financial services. 

I am interested in leveraging impact investing as a means to open the gates of the capital markets to emerging economies and businesses, enabling profitable returns and large-scale, sustainable social impact. I want to change the flows of capital to reach startups, entrepreneurs, markets, and sectors that are ignored or undervalued by traditional investors. 

Demonstrated ability to connect with clients and partners, diagnose key needs, and deliver effective solutions. Significant international work experience and cross-cultural understanding.

Specialties: Strategy development and execution, impact investing, private equity, venture capital, economic development, financial services, private-sector driven international development, non-profit management, education, management consulting, financial inclusion, technology, telecommunications
