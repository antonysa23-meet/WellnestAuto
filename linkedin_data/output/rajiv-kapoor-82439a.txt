Rajiv Kapoor

Founder & Partner at Chai Ventures, a New York City-based pre-seed and seed fund founded in 2019 investing in women-led companies across consumer, health tech/wellness, and other high-growth sectors.

Rajiv's investment track record includes early public-market positions in Tesla, Workday, and Tableau, as well as early-stage private investments in Compt, Hacware, and Nomic.

He holds a BA in Economics from Vanderbilt University and a Master's in Management from London Business School.
