Huge congratulations to Jae Lee, Soyoung Lee, and the entire TwelveLabs team on their $100M Series B co-led by NEA and NAVER Ventures! 🚀🚀

We’ve believed in Twelve Labs from the beginning because we shared a simple conviction: if AI is going to understand the world, it has to understand video.

Video is the richest form of data we have. Yet despite the explosion of AI, most of the world’s video data has remained largely inaccessible to machines.

What Jae and the team have built is not just another AI application. They are building the foundational models and infrastructure that make video searchable, understandable, and actionable at scale. As Jae has said, text tells you something happened; video proves it.

Every company with video - from media and sports to security, healthcare, education, and enterprise operations - needs a way to turn vast libraries of video into structured, searchable intelligence. Every enterprise needs to be exploring what Twelve Labs can do today!

Congratulations again to the entire team on this well-deserved milestone. As the team has said from day one- the future of AI will be multimodal, and Twelve Labs is helping define that future. 🚀

🚀 Big congrats to Don Muir and the entire F2 team on announcing their $14M seed round, bringing total funding to $24M in just a few months!

We invested on day one because we believe one of the biggest opportunities in AI isn’t replacing knowledge workers. It’s enabling them to do significantly more with the same team.

Private credit firms and banks process enormous amounts of information across data rooms, PDFs, spreadsheets, underwriting memos, and historical deal work. Yet much of that knowledge remains fragmented across systems and difficult to leverage efficiently.

F2 is changing that.

The company transforms deal materials into structured intelligence that helps investment teams move faster across screening, underwriting, and portfolio monitoring. Their newest products, Institutional Knowledge and Adam, help firms retain context from prior deals and apply those insights to future investment decisions.

As AI becomes embedded into core financial workflows, we believe the winners will be the companies building vertical systems designed around how industries actually operate, not generic tools that require teams to change how they work.

Excited to continue supporting F2 and the team as they build!

🚀🚀🚀Huge congratulations to the Nova Intelligence team on their Series A led by Chemistry and Seed led by Accel! 🚀🚀

Grateful that Position Ventures was the very first check into Nova, and so thrilled to see the incredible traction the team has built since day one.

Nova is building agentic AI for SAP, one of the largest and most mission-critical categories in enterprise software.

SAP powers the core operations of the world’s largest companies across finance, payroll, supply chain, manufacturing, procurement, and logistics. These systems are incredibly complex, deeply customized, and dependent on scarce expert knowledge.

The company is starting with a massive forcing function: the S/4HANA migration wave. Thousands of enterprises need to modernize aging SAP environments, reduce technical debt, and migrate critical workflows without disrupting the business.

But migration is just the wedge.

The bigger opportunity is the full SAP lifecycle: development, testing, documentation, modernization, production issue resolution, and day-to-day operations.

This is what I love most about Nova: they are not building AI for a theoretical use case.

They are building AI for one of the most complex, expensive, and mission-critical workflows in the global economy.

Huge congratulations again to Emma Qian, Sam Yang, Prof. Dr. Alexander Zeier, and the entire Nova team. We are so excited to be part of the journey!!
