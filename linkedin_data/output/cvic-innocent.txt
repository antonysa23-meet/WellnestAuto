National security space is not only won in orbit. It is won in the industrial base.

That was my core takeaway from today’s Space Industry Panel at the Northeast National Security Conference in Nashua.

The Northeast has the ingredients: advanced manufacturing, defense primes, universities, robotics, composites, propulsion, launch (soon), sensing, and a deep culture of practical engineering. But if we want this region to matter in the national security space, we have to help companies cross the gap from prototype to production.

I was glad to represent bluShift Aerospace alongside Jon Beam of Rogue Aerospace, Joshua Haines of RTX, and Randy Makee of the NH Aerospace & Defense Consortium. Thank you to NH Tech Alliance for bringing this ecosystem together.

Resilience starts on the ground.
