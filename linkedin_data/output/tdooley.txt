Tracy Dooley, MD

Partner at Avestria Ventures, investing in early-stage medtech, healthtech, and life-science companies focused on women's health and female founders.

Tracy holds an MD from Stanford University and a BS in Biomedical Engineering from USC. Her career spans healthcare M&A investment banking, serving as Head of Global Operations at Novartis Diagnostics/Grifols, and digital health/innovation consulting for clients including the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation and Novartis.

She is a member of the Women's Health Executive Committee at Springboard Enterprises.
