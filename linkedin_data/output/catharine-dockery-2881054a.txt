VENTURE CAPITAL DINNER

Catharine Dockery is the Founder and General Partner of Vice Ventures, an early-stage venture capital fund investing at the Seed and Series A stages. The firm typically writes ~$500K checks into companies in sectors often overlooked by traditional investors, including cannabis, alcohol, nicotine alternatives, psychedelics, gambling, and sexual wellness.

Chuck Pettid is a venture investor and executive at Republic, a global investment platform that enables individuals and institutions to invest in private startups and alternative assets. He focuses on sourcing and evaluating early-stage companies across sectors like fintech, consumer internet, gaming, and emerging technologies, helping connect founders with capital through Republic’s investment platform.

Chris G. is a venture investor at Align Ventures, an early-stage venture capital firm backing founders building technology and consumer brands shaping the future of commerce. He works with entrepreneurs from the earliest stages, helping them raise capital, refine strategy, and scale their companies while connecting them to Align’s network of operators, investors, and industry leaders. Consumer, Retail tech, early stage, 500k-2M check sizes

Matt Brennan is a Partner at Strata Capital Partners, a technology investment firm backing capital-efficient B2B and vertical AI software companies in industries like healthcare, industrials, energy, and chemicals. The firm typically invests in companies with $2–10M in revenue that have raised under $5M in outside capital and are ready to scale.

Ope Runsewe works with Amex Ventures, the venture capital arm of American Express, where he focuses on building partnerships between the firm, venture investors, and emerging startups. Amex Ventures invests from Seed to Series B in areas such as fintech, payments, commerce infrastructure, enterprise software, and data security.

Micaela Mangot is the Vice President of Global Business Development at Samarian Group, a New York private investment and advisory firm focused on private placements and strategic finance. She works with investors and founders to source opportunities, build partnerships, and support capital formation across global markets.

Vaughn Crowe is the Co-Founder and Managing Partner of NVP Capital, a New York venture capital firm that invests at the seed stage in enterprise software and vertical AI companies across industries like healthcare, financial services, logistics, and workforce management. The firm typically invests up to about $3M per company and helps founders scale by connecting them with enterprise customers and strategic partners.
