Marissa Fayer

Partner and Investment Committee member at Goddess Gaia Ventures, a London-based venture capital fund founded in 2021 focused on women's health, wellness, and sports.

Marissa is also CEO & Founder of the nonprofit HERhealthEQ and CEO of DeepLook Medical. She has more than 20 years of experience as a medtech executive and entrepreneur.

She is a TEDx speaker, a UCSC Miller Center Social Entrepreneur Fellow, and has been named to the Top 100 Women in MedTech list as well as a 2024 "MedTech Voice to Watch."
