📣 Big news from Hyperlayer.
 
We’ve raised £30 million (US$40 million) to accelerate our mission of helping banks and businesses launch future-proof financial products faster.
 
This round is one of the largest early-stage European fintech raises since 2024, led by global equity specialist CDAM (UK) Limited, with participation from Mouro Capital, Iona Star, Flintlock Capital, and existing investors including Susquehanna International Group (Susquehanna Private Equity Investments, LLLP).
 
🚀 The funding will fuel our product and engineering expansion, support international growth, and help us deliver our plug-and-play financial technology to more banks, wealth managers, and businesses worldwide.
 
💬 “This funding underlines the demand we’re seeing for our breakthrough tech which cuts time-to-launch, costs, and risk for banks and other businesses looking to innovate,” said Rob Rooney, co-founder and CEO.
 
Hyperlayer already powers Tier 1 banks with advanced account features, loyalty programmes, and new wealth tools, giving financial institutions the ability to match and overtake digital-first competitors without replacing core systems.
 
🌍 The next 18 months will see Hyperlayer scaling rapidly across retail and commercial banking, loyalty and rewards, wealth and asset management, and reg-tech markets.
