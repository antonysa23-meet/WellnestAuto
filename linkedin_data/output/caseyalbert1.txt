Casey Albert

Managing Partner at SteelSky Ventures, an Atlanta-based women's-health-tech venture capital fund. SteelSky closed its first fund at $72M in assets under management in 2022, at the time described as the largest women's health-focused fund.

Casey has 20 years of experience in healthcare entrepreneurship and investing. She previously served as CEO & Founder of Kangu, a maternal health access company later acquired by EMC, where she helped increase prenatal service utilization by 80%.
