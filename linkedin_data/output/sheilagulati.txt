Fred Luddy

  • 3rd+

CEO @ StarLifter | Building the Decision Layer of the Enterprise Signal → Context → Decision → Action

2d • 

When I started ServiceNow, the problem was clear: work was fragmented across systems, and people spent too much time managing processes instead of getting work done. We built a workflow platform to help solve that problem.

Today, the next bottleneck is decision-making.

Organizations have invested heavily in ERP, analytics, workflow automation, and now AI. But many important business decisions still require people to collect information from different systems, figure out what's changed, determine what's important, gather in a room to make a decision, and coordinate action across teams. In many ways, people have become the middleware between data and execution.

That's why I started StarLifter.

We're building the Enterprise Decision Layer - a system that continuously understands operational change, reasons about business context, and orchestrates decisions and actions across enterprise systems. The goal isn't to replace people. It's to eliminate the manual work required to understand what's happening and help organizations respond more quickly and consistently.

I'm grateful to our early design partners who are helping us build this from the ground up, my team, and to Sheila Gulati and the team at Tola Capital for leading our seed round. We're looking forward to sharing more as we continue building!

That vision became Reliant AI.
