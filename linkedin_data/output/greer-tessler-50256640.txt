Big news to share today- 
We’re excited to announce that The Marzetti Company has entered into a definitive agreement to acquire Bachan’s for $400M. 
This outcome is a great example of SFV’s core investment thesis in action. The food industry continues to shift toward cleaner, higher-quality products, and Bachan’s has been a standout brand riding that wave with authenticity and momentum.
Since our initial investment in March 2023, we’ve worked with the Bachan’s team to support their performance at Albertsons. Closing a transaction like this in under three years from investment, and under four years from our first fund close, is a testament to both the brand and the strategy.

Huge congratulations to the Bachan’s team and everyone involved! We’re grateful to have been part of the journey!
