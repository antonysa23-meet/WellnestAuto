Lynne Chou O'Keefe

Founder & Managing Partner of Define Ventures, an early-stage digital health venture capital fund with approximately $800M in assets under management.

Previously a Partner at Kleiner Perkins Caufield & Byers. Before that, Lynne spent approximately eight years in operating roles at Abbott Vascular and Guidant, and also worked at Apax Partners (software-focused venture capital) and in M&A at Goldman Sachs.

Lynne's investment track record includes Livongo (NASDAQ: LVGO) and Hims & Hers (NASDAQ: HIMS), and her launch of Define Ventures in 2020 was covered by TechCrunch as a notable new fund focused on digital health innovation.
