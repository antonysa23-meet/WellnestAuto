Judy Fairburn

Co-Founder & Managing Partner of The51 (formerly StandUp Ventures) and NYA Ventures, based in Calgary, Canada. Judy invests in and supports women-led and women-founded companies, building venture capital infrastructure specifically designed to close the funding gap for women entrepreneurs.

Judy holds an MSc and an MBA. She is a mentor at Creative Destruction Lab, a leading venture-focused seed accelerator, and is a Clean50 honouree recognized for contributions to sustainability and clean technology. She is an alumna of the Schulich School of Engineering at the University of Calgary.

Her work centers on building capital pathways for women founders in Canada, with a particular focus on structuring funds and programs that address structural barriers women face in raising venture capital.
