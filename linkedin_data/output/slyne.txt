Big congrats to Eric Kau Allie Melnick and Nick Haft on launching Climatic today! 🚀

Lung health has been treated as reactive for far too long. Most of us only think about our lungs during wildfire smoke, COVID, or when traveling to heavily polluted cities, but in reality, we’re breathing unhealthy air every day. Despite that, prevention has barely entered the wellness conversation. Climatic is changing that.

Their first product, L Max, is a daily lung health solution designed to help clear airways and support easier breathing in under 30 seconds:
• Direct-to-lung delivery
• Clinically tested, naturally derived ingredients
• Built on decades of lung physiology

Early data is compelling: research with Mount Sinai showed >50% improvement in mucus clearance after a single use, and 98% of athletes in testing reported easier breathing during workouts, alongside HRV gains in just weeks.

Access to LMax opens today → climatichealth.com. (Use code FF20 for 20% off your first order)

Check out their Fitt Insider story for more: https://lnkd.in/eSzJcEF4

🚨 ŌURA just announced they surpassed 5.5 million rings sold today. 

From little known Finnish sleep tracker, to global health movement. This company we backed when it was just $20M in sales is going to do $1B in revenue in 2025, doubling for the second consecutive year.

The acceleration is unprecedented: 
2.5 million rings sold in the past year alone — matching in 12 months what took the company 10+ years to achieve previously.

As Board Chair over the past 6 years since our initial investment in 2019, I've watched this Finnish startup transform the consumer health space, one ring at a time. 

But as with all great success, it is certainly NEVER overnight. The real story isn't just in the headline numbers, it is in the impact: 

🏥 It's the 51% of members managing chronic conditions who finally have continuous health insights. 

⛑️ It's the 11% who are healthcare providers themselves, choosing ŌURA for their own health monitoring. 

🌍 It's the trust earned across 150 countries and 4,000 retail locations.

When we @ Forerunner made that first investment, we saw a world where consumers would become CEOs of their own health. Tom Hale and team haven't just delivered on that vision — they've expanded it. Now,  millions of people are taking control of their own health journey -- not just for sleep, but for peak readiness, improving heart health, managing stress, tracking fertility, monitoring sickness, understanding food impacts, and so much more. 

The transformation of healthcare starts with each of us having the power to make proactive changes in our daily lives. And it starts with accurate data we can trust, in our own hands. 💍 It starts #WithOura.

Congratulations to Nick Ornitz, Shannon Kay and the Topline Pro team on their $27M Series B, led by Northzone 🎉 ! 

At BBG Ventures, we’re excited to back founders building for overlooked segments of the workforce. While 90% of software investment targets knowledge workers, deskless workers and solopreneurs -- like the 2.5M+ skilled tradespeople in the U.S. -- have been left without modern tools. Topline Pro is changing that -- building a vertical AI platform for these business owners, starting with marketing, lead-gen and an AI-sales assistant -- on their way to building out autonomous AI assistants for tradespeople  while they’re out in the field.  

Since our first check in 2021, we’ve been impressed by Nick and Shannon’s commitment to staying close to customers -- a mindset that’s helped pros book $655M+ in revenue to date. We’re thrilled to continue supporting this fantastic team in their next phase of growth. Onward!
