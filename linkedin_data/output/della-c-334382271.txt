About
With an academic foundation from the University of Waterloo and Wilfrid Laurier University, my journey led to the inception of LingoTok, an AI-powered platform transforming video consumption into language learning opportunities. At LingoTok, my role as Founder and CEO centers around leveraging artificial intelligence to create engaging, personalized educational content. Together, we're committed to making learning more accessible and enjoyable, ensuring our efforts resonate with our audience's need for a seamless integration of education and entertainment.

🌟 Looking for Volunteers: Build an AI-Powered Lost Cat Recovery Platform (Non-Profit)
Hi everyone — I’m an AI entrepreneur (ex-consulting & product lead) and a devastated cat owner. After my beloved cat went missing for 3 months in the U.S., I realized how fragmented and inefficient lost pet recovery still is — even in 2025.

Now I want to build something better — a non-profit AI platform to help lost cats reunite with their humans, faster and smarter.

⸻

🛠️ What we’re building (v1.0):
	1.	Cat-Specific Recognition Models: Owners can upload 50–100+ photos to train a personalized visual AI model to identify their cat — not just “a gray cat”.
	2.	Cross-platform Monitoring: Scrape and analyze local shelters, clinics, humane centers, and lost&found boards for possible matches.
	3.	Home Surveillance Scanning: Process home security footage from doorbells, neighbors, or apartment areas to detect cats.
	4.	Geo-Behavioral Prediction: Use Google Maps data to map shrubbery, roads, buildings to suggest search zones.
	5.	AI Support Agent: A chatbot that gives emotional support and generates actionable next steps (call chip company, post on local groups, etc.)

⸻

💡 Why this matters:
	•	Over 400,000 cats/dogs go missing in the U.S. each year
	•	Most shelters don’t scan chips consistently, and many owners lose the golden 72-hour window
	•	Scam posts, outdated websites, and scattered platforms leave people helpless
	•	Typical reward for a lost cat: $500–1000 — people want to pay for real help
	•	AI can bridge this gap. No one’s done it right yet. We want to try.

⸻

🙌 Looking for part-time volunteers (non-profit for now):
	•	Backend / Computer Vision Engineers (Python, YOLO, TensorFlow, etc.)
	•	Frontend / Mobile (React / Flutter preferred)
	•	AI researchers (few-shot learning, anomaly detection, matching)
	•	Product / BD / Outreach (esp. shelter or rescue orgs)
	•	Anyone with pet loss experience or passion for animal welfare

I’m bootstrapping this personally and have an internal AI team from my day job. We’re looking for kind, curious collaborators who want to use tech for good. 💛

📩 DM me if you’re interested — especially if you’ve ever lost a pet.
We’ll do this together. For them. 🐾… more
