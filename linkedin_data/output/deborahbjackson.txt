Deborah Buresh Jackson

Founder & CEO of Plum Alley Investments, founded in 2011 and reorganized in 2017, an early-stage venture firm backing STEM and breakthrough-technology companies with at least one female founder.

Deborah is a former Goldman Sachs banker with more than 21 years of experience as a Wall Street investment banker. She holds an MBA/finance background from Columbia Business School, graduating in 1980.
