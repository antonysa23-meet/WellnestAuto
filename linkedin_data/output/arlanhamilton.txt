About
I am the founder and managing partner of Backstage Capital, a seed investment fund that backs overachieving, underrepresented startup founders. 

I am also a live music production coordinator and tour manager.

You can find me on Medium, Twitter, and Instagram as @ARLANwashere.

I’m excited to share some big news about my evolving role at Backstage Capital and our next chapter of growth. After years of being deeply involved in the day-to-day operations, I’ve decided to transition into a chairperson and advisory position. 

This move will allow me to focus on what I do best—amplifying Backstage’s mission, hosting office hours with founders & investors, chairing the advisory board, and bringing new opportunities to the table through my platform. My goal has always been for Backstage to become an institution that thrives beyond me, and this transition is a step in that direction.

I’m equally thrilled to announce our new strategic partnership with 360 Venture Collective, spearheaded by the incomparable, Kelly O’Connell!

They will be acquiring a significant stake in Backstage’s management entity and joining us to co-manage operations, provide capital, and help us scale. 

Together, we’re launching a $200M fund in 2025😍 —a game-changing opportunity to invest with even greater conviction and impact.

Additionally, we’re stepping away from the traditional micro fund approach and leaning into conviction-based investments—opportunities that have a clear path to strategic acquisitions or public market exits. This shift lets us go deeper on historically undercapitalized sectors while staying sharp and aligned with what the market needs right now.

I want to take a moment to highlight the incredible work of Brittany Davis and Natalia Napoleon De Bens, who have been instrumental in driving Backstage forward. Their leadership and expertise on the Backstage side are critical to this next chapter, and I’m confident that with their continued contributions, along with 360’s partnership, we’ll achieve even more together.

This partnership ensures Backstage remains sustainable while multiplying our ability to support underestimated founders and create a lasting legacy. I’m confident this next chapter will take us to incredible heights, and I’m honored to continue being part of the journey in this new capacity.

YOLO! :)

This was the first time in 9 years that I’ve allowed anyone to film me walking around the airport where I used to sleep while homeless before launching Backstage Capital. 

You may have heard me speak about it, but you haven’t seen me take you back to the spot. 

This was on purpose.

I still have trauma from that time and the years leading up to it. I know that it inspires people and therefore, I will never stop telling the story. But I always knew that to relive it in a way, would be tough. 

ABC News was absolutely wonderful and respectful. I’m so grateful to have told my story—in this way—to them. 

This aired on several stations across the country this week and is available across streaming platforms. Thank you to new followers who have reached out! 

And for those of you who have been rockin with me for a while now, I appreciate you dearly.
