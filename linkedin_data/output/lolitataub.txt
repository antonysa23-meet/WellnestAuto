About
Hey, I'm Lolita. I'm a Latina venture capitalist, operator-turned-investor, and global startup ecosystem builder. I run Ganas Ventures, where I invest $100K checks in pre-seed and seed, community-driven software startups. I've spent nearly 20 years in tech, venture capital, and early-stage ecosystems, across big tech (IBM + Cisco), startups, and VC. I've invested in 100+ startups globally, with a meaningful focus on women, BIPOC, and LGBTQ founders, and previously worked with Lightspeed, Backstage Capital, K Fund, Indie.vc, and Portfolia. I also co-founded The Community Fund. Select investments include Brex + AngelList.
 
🧐 INVESTMENT PHILOSOPHY: Great companies aren't built in isolation. Community is capital, networks are infrastructure, and trust compounds globally.
 
💰 HOW I INVEST + SUPPORT FOUNDERS
 1. Access + clarity over exclusivity + pedigree
2. Long-term alignment over hype cycles
3. On-the-ground learning over second-hand assumptions

🎉 Happy New Year! We're kicking off the year with our Ganas Ventures Global Tour Field Report. 

The Ganas Global Tour confirmed something many founders already know: venture capital is still mapped to the past and we need to get with the now. Across Asia, MENA, Eastern Europe - and informed by years of work in the US and Latin America - we kept seeing the same thing: global-first, AI-native founders building real businesses well outside legacy hubs. 

This final field report pulls together what’s changed, what hasn’t, and where the real arbitrage is starting to show up. 

Huge thank you to the founders, investors, and ecosystem builders who made this tour possible. Your openness, generosity and local insight shaped this far more than any desk research ever could.
