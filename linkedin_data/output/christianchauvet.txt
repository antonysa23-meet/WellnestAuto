Christian Chauvet

Partner at Lee Equity Partners, a New York-based middle-market private equity firm focused on financial and healthcare services, including fertility clinic rollups. He joined the firm in 2014 and focuses on healthcare services investing.

Christian has led or played a significant role in 11 platform investments representing more than $1 billion in invested capital, spanning behavioral health, physician services, post-acute care, and payor services. He is also affiliated with Lee Equity portfolio company Solaris Health Partners.

Previously, he was a Business Analyst at McKinsey & Company. He holds a BA in Government from Harvard College, graduating magna cum laude, and was named one of GrowthCap's "40 Under 40 Growth Investors of 2025."
