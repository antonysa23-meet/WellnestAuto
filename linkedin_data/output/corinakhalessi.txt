Corina Khalessi

Associated with Coyote Ventures, a seed-stage digital health fund investing in women's health and other overlooked health populations. Corina has been featured on the HLTH conference member directory and appeared on a "Beyond Babies" women's health roundtable panel discussing reproductive and maternal health innovation.

She is also listed among staff/board affiliates of Collective Power for Reproductive Justice, reflecting a background connecting healthcare investing with reproductive rights and justice advocacy work.

Note: Coyote Ventures' own team page lists a "Corina Leu" (Duke MBA, previously at GSK and Planned Parenthood) in a similar venture-partner role; this may be the same person under a different professional name, though this has not been independently confirmed.
