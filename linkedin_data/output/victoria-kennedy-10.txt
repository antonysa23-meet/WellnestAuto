I started Patternist because I saw the gap between what capital could do and what it is doing. We’re starting to close that gap by bringing together who aren't waiting for the future but building new economies and new systems now.

We are doing our FIRST event in LA May 6th! "We Build Anyway: Creating Our Future on Our Terms" is the next iteration of a part of our bi-monthly event series! This isn’t your typical panel. It’s a gathering of builders who are designing regenerative models of ownership, aligning capital with justice, and investing in infrastructure that supports resilience and collective wellbeing.

If you’re trying to build differently—this is your room. And if you are interested in working with Patternist, let’s connect! Join us https://lu.ma/nb7rmz1o
