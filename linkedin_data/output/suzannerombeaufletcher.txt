1mo • Edited • 

I am so incredibly excited to share what we have been building over the last few months. 

It is finally time to make the internet benefit-ready. 🚀

Every year, American workers hand back billions of dollars in unspent funds. Why? Because the $200B+ benefit economy—from HSAs and FSAs to lifestyle rewards and employer perks—has never been treated as a modern consumer product. 

We expect everyday people to study convoluted rulebooks just to spend the money they've already earned. 

As a serial founder who has spent a decade obsessing over consumer experience in global e-commerce, I lived this friction firsthand a few years ago when navigating unexpected health needs for my own family. Even with my background, I was completely lost. 

We don't need another clunky corporate portal. We need to meet consumers exactly where they already are. 

Today, we are officially launching Caeli—the ultimate benefit-maximizing agent for everyone. 

Caeli is a free browser extension that embeds benefit intelligence directly into your everyday shopping. 

Watch our launch video below to see how Caeli gives Mia her new shopping superpower. 😉

Under the hood, it’s heavy-lift agentic AI. But to you, it’s an effortless application that gives you a financial advantage in seconds. That’s what AI done right should feel like. 

Perhaps the next big tech milestone isn’t just another massive model, but a mighty application that makes a working American’s life just a little bit better  

Update: For everyone asking where to download it, you can grab the free Caeli extension directly 🚀 in the Chrome Web Store 👉 https://lnkd.in/giVNuaaW

For more than a decade, mobile app developers have been locked into app stores as the only path to distribution - surrendering up to 30% of revenue and competing in an increasingly expensive acquisition environment.

That model is starting to crack.

With richer messaging now live across both iOS and Android, the inbox has become a programmable, interactive surface that works consistently across devices. For the first time, apps can live where people already spend their time.

Today, we’re launching Jest - the first marketplace for messaging apps.

Games on Jest start instantly inside a conversation. No downloads. No app store friction. Just tap and play.

Here are the highlights from the last 4 months of beta:
 • 1M+ apps and games played during beta
 • 3-4x higher retention than traditional mobile apps
 • 35-60% lower acquisition costs

We’ve also launched the Jest Apps Fund, deploying $40K - $1M per app to back the first generation of messaging franchises. If you are building the next breakout franchise and want a new surface to launch on, while keeping 90% of revenue, let’s talk.
