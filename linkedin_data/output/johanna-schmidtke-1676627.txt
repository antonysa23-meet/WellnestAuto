Who will you see at the ADI Forum?

We at ADI Analytics founded the ADI Forum in 2017 with a simple idea: the energy landscape is more integrated than before, and real opportunities emerge where segments meet and how they interact.

That’s why the Forum has consistently drawn a diverse audience spanning operators in upstream, midstream, refining, chemicals, and utilities.

But the ADI Forum community also includes colleagues from companies in EPC, OFSE, and industrial sectors that serve the energy and chemical markets.

Private equity and venture capital investors, early-stage companies, and academia and national labs are also represented at the ADI Forum.

We’ve been fortunate to convene these diverse stakeholders — often senior industry executives and experienced professionals from strategy, planning, M&A, and business development functions — because of the ADI Forum’s singular focus on one thing:

Providing a strategic perspective across the energy and chemical value chains.

If you believe in our thesis that the energy landscape is more integrated than before, then understanding where the segments meet and how they interact is critical to uncovering opportunities and value.

Facilitating that strategic, value chain–wide perspective is why ADI Analytics — whose consulting work spans the value chains in oil & gas, energy, and chemicals — started hosting the ADI Forum back in 2017.

I hope you’ll join us on January 28, 2026 in Houston.
