Irene Hrusovsky, MD

Managing Director at Belle Capital USA, an early-stage angel fund investing in female entrepreneurs. She also serves as General Manager of IGH Ventures, LLC, and sits on the investment committees of Belle Capital and Phenomenelle Angels.

Irene is a board-certified pediatrician, holding a BS in Biochemistry and Genetics and an MD from the University of British Columbia. She has more than 30 years of business and medical leadership experience, including over 20 years at Abbott Laboratories in pharma, medical device, and diagnostics executive roles, and VP roles at GlaxoSmithKline.

She served as Chairman, President, and CEO of EraGen Biosciences, a molecular diagnostics company, leading it to a successful acquisition exit in 2011.
